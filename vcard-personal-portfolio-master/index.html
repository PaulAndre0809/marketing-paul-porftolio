About me

I'm a Digital Marketing Manager based in Dubai specializing in performance marketing, campaign coordination, and marketing automation.

At Gilani Mobility and We Aspire, I manage and optimize Meta Ads campaigns, coordinate marketing execution across content and advertising teams, and oversee lead generation workflows. I also implemented ManyChat automation to filter Meta Ads leads and automatically route qualified inquiries to the sales team.

My role includes training team members on Meta Ads campaign management, preparing monthly marketing performance reports for management, and analyzing KPIs to improve campaign performance.

I also coordinate content production and marketing activations, including product shoots, promotional campaigns, and brand participation in events such as the AccessAbilities Expo and collaborations with Dubai Festival City Mall.

My focus is building marketing systems that combine creative content, automation, and data-driven campaign performance.
