# about

About me

I’m a Marketing Manager responsible for leading and executing digital marketing initiatives for mobility and education brands. My work focuses on coordinating campaigns, managing creative production, and overseeing marketplace operations across platforms such as Amazon, Noon, and Carrefour.

At Gilani Mobility and We Aspire, I manage the marketing team and ensure campaigns move from strategy to execution—overseeing content creation, social media, product listings, and promotional campaigns while coordinating designers, video editors, and digital specialists.

My role is to turn marketing ideas into structured execution, ensuring campaigns, content, and marketplace listings are delivered efficiently and aligned with business goals.
